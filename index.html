Feature

Bomberbot Parents is an MVP of a tool that allows parents to follow their children's performance, understand what they are learning and how they are doing it. All this in real time and from anywhere, as long as they have an Internet connection.

Currently, Bomberbot does not have a tool that allows parents to monitor their children's performance. That is why this solution has been proposed, in order to provide full access to this information and with the possibility of downloading these reports in pdf format.

Another feature presented by Bomberbot Parents is that it displays motivational or alert messages according to the performance of the children, which can be managed through the administrator module, which allows Bomberbot staff to edit them.
